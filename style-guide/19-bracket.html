About this direction

19 — Bracket

The site becomes bracket season. Because Spring Reign is the world's largest mixed youth ultimate tournament, the whole DiscNW season is seeded like a March-Madness tree that draws itself round by round as you scroll. Matchup cards slide in, illustrative UPSET ALERTs detonate, the road-to-the-final rail fills, the counters tally the real numbers, and a live countdown runs to the next first pull. At the top of the tree, the formline mark is crowned as the champion. Even with animation off, the bracket shows fully drawn with every number and date already correct — calm and complete, nothing ticking or shaking.

Signature — the live bracket

A two-region, single-elimination tournament tree drawn as a clean diagram: the connector wires ink themselves one at a time, cascading from the semifinals toward the final. Seeded matchup cards carry DiscNW's real programs, reveal a scouting report on hover or focus, and two lower seeds bust the bracket with an UPSET ALERT — screen-shake plus an expanding gold shockwave. The road-to-the-final rail tracks your progress; the champion node crowns Spring Reign under the white formline crest.

Motion — bracket mania

One easing language: a snap for micro moves, a lux curve for wire draws and reveals, and a pop overshoot for cards and the champion landing. Kinetic marquees run opposite directions, a broadcast LIVE bug pulses, and the countdown ticks in tabular numerals. Everything runs smoothly and stays light; the base state is the finished bracket.

Type

Anton — the blocky poster display for the mega-type, round labels, scores and champion. Archivo (700–900) for the matchup cards and body. Spline Sans Mono for seeds, timecodes, kickers and the countdown.

Choose this if

You want the loudest, most event-driven front door in the kit — one that turns the DiscNW season into a bracket the whole city fills out, and makes registering feel like claiming a spot in the tournament. It is unmistakably its own, and it is earned — because DiscNW actually runs the world's largest mixed youth ultimate tournament.

Think twice if

You need a calm, dense utility homepage first. Bracket mania is a seasonal campaign skin, loud by design; its energy can overwhelm the standings, schedules and rainout tools that the grounded directions handle better underneath this voice.
