About this direction

16 — Highlight Reel

Ultimate is thrilling, so this direction opens the site like the nightly SportsCenter Top 10 and lets the plays sell the sport. The whole page is a highlight show: a scroll-driven countdown from #10 to #1, each moment full-bleed under a giant ghosted rank numeral and a broadcast lower-third that slams and wipes in. A persistent score “bug” rides the corner the whole way down, badged with the DiscNW formline mark as the station ident. Loud on purpose, and still easy to read.

Palette — broadcast night
Broadcast Black #0A0B0D Broadcast White #F6F7F9 Fastbreak Red #FF4655 Press Grey #9AA0A8
Type
Anton — the heavy condensed sports face doing ranks, the mega-headline, and every lower-third title at broadcast scale. Inter — captions, metadata, and body, with tabular figures on every clock and score.
Signature
The Top 10 countdown: ten full-bleed moments (two video loops at #1 and #2, the most dramatic Spring Reign stills for the rest, one self-drawing Callahan diagram), each with a colossal ghost rank numeral and a lower-third that wipes in on the overshoot curve — and it all still reads with the scripts off.
Motion lane — broadcast energy
Ranks slam in (--ease-pop overshoot), lower-thirds wipe (--ease-snap), reveals rise (--ease-lux). Autoplay muted loops, a hover replay-scrub on the video moments, twin kinetic marquees, a live score bug with a running clock, and a Spring Reign countdown — all of it kept light and smooth.
Deep brand moment
The white formline mark is the network ident inside the persistent bug — on a solid dark chrome pill with full clearspace, never sitting on moving video. The real lockup signs the nav and this appendix.
Choose this if
You want a front door that plays like a broadcast — proof, in one scroll, that ultimate is a spectator sport, and that registering is joining the show.
Think twice if
You need the working homepage first. This is the cold open, not the schedule grid — standings, rosters, and account flows still ride one of the grounded directions underneath this voice.
Accessibility & degradation
One h1, semantic landmarks, alt text on every photo, AA contrast on every loud pairing. Without scripts: all ten ranks, captions, posters, stats, and the Spring Reign date are present. Reduced motion: no marquee, no counting, no autoplay — final values, settled, in under 2.5 s.
